# **1\. Executive Summary**

BarBell is a gym-native AI fitness platform built for gym owners, not gym members. Unlike consumer fitness apps that members download independently, BarBell integrates directly into a gym's ecosystem — branded under the gym's identity, aware of the gym's specific equipment, and designed to drive member retention and generate new subscription revenue for the gym operator.

The platform uses location intelligence, adaptive AI coaching, and a tiered subscription model to solve the gym industry's two biggest problems: members who stop showing up and revenue that stops at the membership fee.

BarBell is built to military-grade physical readiness standards — designed for high-performance institutional fitness programs and now being made available to commercial gyms.

# **2\. Problem Statement**

## **The Gym Owner Problem**

Gym owners face two chronic challenges that existing technology has failed to solve:

* Member Retention — The average gym loses 50% of new members within six months. Members join with intention but disengage when they lack structure, accountability, and progression. Gym owners have no scalable tool to keep members engaged between visits.

* Revenue Ceiling — Most gym revenue stops at the membership fee. Upselling personal training is expensive to staff, inconsistent in quality, and inaccessible to most members. There is no scalable way for gym owners to monetize member engagement beyond the base membership.

## **The Market Gap**

Existing AI fitness apps — Fitbod, JuggernautAI, GymStreak, MyTrainer — are consumer-facing products. Members download them personally, the gym gets no benefit, no data, and no revenue. There is no dominant US platform that puts the gym at the center as the operator and beneficiary of AI-powered fitness.

BarBell fills that gap. It is sold to the gym, branded by the gym, and generates revenue for the gym.

# **3\. Product Overview**

## **What BarBell Does**

BarBell is a white-label AI fitness platform that gym operators license and deploy under their own brand. The platform delivers four core capabilities:

**3.1 — Location-Based Member Engagement**

BarBell uses geofencing technology to detect when a gym member is near the gym. When proximity is detected, the app sends a personalized push notification — factoring in real-time gym traffic — nudging the member to check in. Example notification:

*"Hey Marcus — the gym is quieter than usual right now. Good time to get that session in."*

This drives visit frequency without requiring the gym to hire additional staff or run manual outreach campaigns.

**3.2 — Equipment-Specific Workout Programming**

Each gym's equipment inventory is mapped in the BarBell system during onboarding. All workout programming generated by the AI is built around what that specific gym actually has — not generic templates. A member at a CrossFit box gets different programming than a member at a Planet Fitness. The workout is always executable with the equipment in front of them.

**3.3 — Adaptive AI Coaching**

BarBell's AI coach tracks every workout and adapts programming over time based on the individual member's performance, progression, and feedback. As the member improves, the program evolves. The system functions as a personal trainer that never forgets a session, never has an off day, and scales to serve every member in the gym simultaneously.

**3.4 — Tiered Subscription Revenue Model**

BarBell gives gym operators a built-in monetization layer through subscription tiers:

* Base Tier (Free) — Members get access to basic AI-generated workouts, gym check-in alerts, and progress tracking. This tier drives adoption and retention at no cost to the member.

* Standard Tier (Paid) — Members unlock full adaptive coaching, detailed analytics, nutrition guidance integration, and priority features. Gym sets the price.

* Premium Tier (Paid) — Members unlock advanced performance tracking, recovery protocols, goal-specific programming, and premium content. Gym sets the price.

The gym receives a revenue share from all paid tier subscriptions. BarBell becomes a recurring revenue stream that runs independently of the gym's staffing model.

# **5\. Technical Architecture**

## **Platform**

* Mobile App: React Native (iOS \+ Android from single codebase)

* Web Dashboard: React / Next.js

* Backend: Node.js / Express or equivalent API layer

* Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL) — existing TDXI infrastructure

* AI / LLM Layer: Anthropic Claude API for workout generation and adaptive coaching

* Geofencing: Google Maps Platform or native device location services

* Push Notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)

* Payments / Subscriptions: Stripe

* Hosting: Railway (backend), Vercel (web dashboard)

## **Data Model — Core Entities**

* GymOperator — gym profile, branding config, equipment inventory, subscription tiers, revenue data

* Member — profile, goals, fitness level, injury flags, subscription tier, workout history

* WorkoutSession — date, exercises, sets, reps, weights, completion status, AI feedback

* EquipmentInventory — per-gym list of available equipment with category tags

* GeofenceEvent — member ID, gym ID, timestamp, trigger type, notification sent

* Subscription — member ID, tier, billing cycle, status, revenue attribution

## **AI Coaching Engine Logic**

1. Member completes onboarding — goals, level, equipment access captured

2. AI generates initial program based on onboarding data and gym equipment inventory

3. Member completes sessions — performance data logged per set

4. After each session, AI evaluates performance against expected output

5. Program adjusts volume, intensity, and exercise selection based on trend data

6. Weekly check-in prompt surfaces to member — subjective feedback fed back into model

# **8\. Revenue Model**

## **B2B SaaS — Gym Operator Licensing**

BarBell charges gym operators a monthly platform fee based on gym size and tier selection. Suggested pricing structure:

| Starter (up to 200 members) | $299/month — includes white-label app, basic AI programming, operator dashboard |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Growth (up to 500 members)** | $599/month — includes full adaptive coaching, geofencing, tiered subscriptions |
| **Pro (500+ members / chains)** | $999+/month — includes all features, multi-location, dedicated onboarding support |

## **Revenue Share — Member Subscriptions**

Gym operators set their own pricing for member subscription tiers. BarBell takes a 20% revenue share on all member subscription payments processed through the platform. The gym keeps 80%.

## **Future Revenue Streams**

* Institutional / Government contracts — Military branch fitness programs, corporate wellness programs

* Data insights packages — Anonymized performance trend reports for gym operators

* Trainer marketplace — Certified trainers available as an upgrade through the platform
